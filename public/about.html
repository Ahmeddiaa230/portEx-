Our Story

Founded over a decade ago in Istanbul, PortEx began with a simple vision: to make international freight accessible, transparent, and efficient for businesses of all sizes.

Today, we operate as a full-spectrum logistics partner — handling everything from LCL sea freight and air cargo to complex multi-modal shipments requiring customs clearance across multiple jurisdictions.

Our Istanbul base gives us a strategic advantage at the crossroads of Europe, the Middle East, and Central Asia — one of the world's most active trade corridors.
